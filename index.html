ACES was born with a vision to challenge the shortcomings of textbook learning and explore new technologies in a comfortable environment. The entire executive board of ACES exemplifies a commitment to this vision and is working relentlessly toward it. Adhering to its vision ACES is working towards organizing tons of sessions and talks to incorporate the idea of building things and learning new technologies along with gaining experience. We aim to provide a guiding space to explore a whole new world of technologies. To evolve as a complete engineer one needs to let their creativity show. The mission of ACES is to provide a friendly atmosphere for students in Cusat to code, connect, create and bring their ideas to life.
